About Questify

Questify is your AI-powered tool for generating customized quizzes, tests, and learning materials.

Questify enables educators, students, and content creators to automatically generate multiple-choice questions (MCQs), question-answer pairs, fill-in-the-blank questions, and true/false questions from your documents. It supports PDF, DOCX, and TXT files.

Simply upload your text, select the type of questions you'd like to generate, and let Questify do the work. It's fast, easy, and powerful!
